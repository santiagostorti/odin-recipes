Homemade Pizza Recipe

Description

There is nothing quite like a fresh, homemade pizza straight from the oven. This recipe features a crispy yet chewy crust, a savory tomato sauce, and perfectly melted mozzarella cheese.

Making pizza at home allows you to control every ingredient, ensuring a much tastier and healthier result than any delivery. It’s a fun and rewarding process that will bring the authentic taste of Italy to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni or your favorite toppings
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Olive oil for brushing the crust
  • Cornmeal (optional, for the baking sheet)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. If using a baking sheet, sprinkle it with cornmeal to prevent sticking and transfer the dough.
  4. Spread an even layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce.
  6. Add your toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.) and sprinkle with oregano.
  7. Brush the outer crust with a little olive oil for a golden finish.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
